Baked Tomato Slices Recipe

Description

Baked tomato slices are a simple, flavorful side dish made by slicing fresh tomatoes and layering them on a baking sheet. They're typically seasoned with salt, pepper, herbs like oregano or basil, and topped with breadcrumbs or grated cheese such as Parmesan for extra texture and flavor. After a quick bake in the oven until tender and slightly crisp on top, theyâ€™re served warm as a light appetizer or a complement to main dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 1 large tomato, cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves stripped and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush a baking sheet with about 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  2. Arrange tomato slices on the baking sheet; sprinkle rosemary and garlic on top. Brush with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
